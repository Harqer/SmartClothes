Sensitivity is validated at the picotesla scale, sufficient for cardiac biomagnetic fields of 50–100 pT at the chest wall.

Material Architecture

Five layers.
One fiber. Infinite cycles.

Each NanoSilk Quantum Sensor Fiber (NQSF) is a coaxial multilayer structure — 60 to 80 microns in diameter — assembled from quantum-active and encapsulant materials onto a biological silk fibroin core.

L1
Structural Core
Bombyx mori Silk Fibroin — 50–60 µm
Wet-spun protein fiber providing the mechanical backbone (300–740 MPa tensile strength). Natural hydroxyl and amine surface groups bond subsequent layers without primer treatment. Wet tensile strength increases upon hydration — uniquely suited for wash-cycle environments.
L2
Inner Encapsulant / Dielectric
Hexagonal Boron Nitride (hBN) — 5–20 nm CVD film
A 5.9–6.0 eV bandgap insulator that electrically isolates sensing layers, routes NV fluorescence as a photonic waveguide, and mechanically locks the graphene twist angle above it via a 900 GPa in-plane modulus. Chemically inert across the full detergent pH range.
L3
Quantum Magnetometer
NV-Center Nanodiamond / PMMA Ring — 100–300 nm
Six nitrogen-vacancy centers in hexagonal geometry deliver full 3D vector magnetic field reconstruction at each sensing pixel. Room-temperature sensitivity validated at ~18 pT/√Hz in ensemble configurations. Diamond Mohs hardness 10 — completely unaffected by wash agitation, water, or detergent chemistry.
L4
Quantum Hall Sensor
Magic-Angle Twisted Bilayer Graphene — 0.7 nm + hBN cap
Two CVD graphene monolayers stacked at exactly 1.1° produce a topological flat-band state. Spontaneous time-reversal symmetry breaking generates a quantized Hall resistance that is perturbed by biomagnetic fields — enabling passive cardiac sensing via impedance modulation readable by a standard NFC interrogation field.
L5
Outer Encapsulant
PDMS / SiO₂ Superhydrophobic Sheath — 3–8 µm
A hierarchical micro-nano surface structure achieves a 150–156° water contact angle (superhydrophobic Cassie-Baxter state), preventing water ingress to inner quantum layers during immersion and spin-cycle washing. FDA-approved, skin-safe, chemically stable across pH 1–14. No degradation mechanism exists under ISO 6330 conditions.
Total fiber diameter 60–80 µm places the NQSF within the dimensional range of fine natural silk thread, enabling seamless integration into standard loom or knitting machine production. No modification to existing garment manufacturing infrastructure is required.
Operational Mission Fit

Built for contested environments

Existing biosensor solutions impose logistical burden, increase electromagnetic signature, and fail under sustained operational conditions. The NQSF system was architected to eliminate every one of these failure modes.

Aircrew Physiological Monitoring

Continuous cardiac, respiratory, and cognitive-load proxies for aircrew in high-G and hypoxic flight profiles — without any electrode, patch, or device that interferes with flight equipment or emergency egress.

SOF Operator Digital Twin

Real-time physiological state vectors transmitted to C2 systems give commanders visibility into operator readiness, fatigue, and stress load during sustained operations — without increasing the warfighter's gear burden by a single gram.

Medical Readiness & Triage

Persistent biomagnetic and thermoregulatory data streams enable early identification of physiological deterioration — providing medics and flight surgeons with objective, pre-symptom indicators without requiring additional medical devices on the patient.

Low Observable / Low Signature

Zero on-garment electronics means zero active RF emission from the sensor platform. The inductive reader can be operated in directed, short-range coupling mode — producing a signature-controlled interrogation compatible with emissions-controlled (EMCON) environments.

Sustained Logistics Advantage

Indefinite machine washability eliminates the field-maintenance burden imposed by every competing biosensor platform. No battery replacement cycle, no electrode replenishment, no calibration interval — the system readiness clock starts at zero and stays there.

OMEN / CAL Integration

Physiological digital twin state vectors are structured as Cursor-on-Target (CoT) XML or JSON objects, directly ingestible by the OMEN Common Analytic Layer. Each packet carries timestamp, unit ID, zone, physiological metrics, and inter-pixel confidence scoring — no integration middleware required.
